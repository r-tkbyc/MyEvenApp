Getting Started

Welcome

A classic number sliding puzzle tuned for the Even Realities G2. Scroll to pick a tile, tap to slide, and race toward an ordered board.

Launch

Open EvenSlidingPuzzle from Even Hub. You'll see the title screen on your glasses with a list of grid modes.

Display

  • Left: Mode label, move counter, and control hints
  • Right: The square board with numbered tiles
  • Focus: The currently selectable tile is shown in inverted colors
Game Modes

3x3 Puzzle

Eight tiles numbered 1–8 plus one blank. Quick to pick up, friendly for warm-ups and casual play.

4x4 Puzzle

Fifteen tiles numbered 1–15 plus one blank. The classic challenge — a proper test of planning.

Solvability: Every board is shuffled from the solved state using random legal moves, so every puzzle you start is guaranteed to be solvable.
Controls

Three gestures on your G2 touchpad run the whole game:

Scroll Swipe up or down to cycle the focus through adjacent movable tiles (or through menu items on the title and menu screens).
Tap Slide the focused tile into the blank space, select a grid size, or confirm a menu choice.
Double Tap Open the in-game menu while playing (Resume, Reset, Title).

Making a Move

  1. Scroll to cycle through the tiles next to the blank
  2. Tap to slide that tile into the blank
  3. Repeat until the board reads 1, 2, 3 … in order
Rules

Objective

Arrange every tile in ascending order, with the blank space in the bottom-right corner. The target layout for 3x3 is:

1 2 3 / 4 5 6 / 7 8 _

Legal Moves

  • Only tiles directly adjacent to the blank can slide
  • Each tap counts as one move on the Moves counter
  • There is no time limit — take your time to plan multi-step sequences

Solved State

When the tiles are in order, the board outline thickens and a ★ Solved! line appears next to your final move count. Double-tap to open the menu and pick a new game.

Menu Options

Double-tap during play to open the in-game menu. Scroll to browse, tap to confirm.

Back

Return to the current puzzle and keep playing. Your move count is preserved.

Reset

Reshuffle the current grid size from scratch. Move count starts at zero.

Title

Return to the title screen to pick a different grid size.

Tips

Solving Strategy

  • Solve the top row first, then the left column, then treat the remaining sub-grid as a smaller puzzle
  • On 4x4, finish each row and column in pairs — this avoids the classic "last three tiles" trap
  • Count your taps: every scroll-to-focus that doesn't land on the tile you wanted is a free move saved

Navigation

  • The focus cycles only through tiles adjacent to the blank, so scrolling is always short
  • Double-tap is reserved for the menu — a single tap never opens it
  • From the title screen, a single tap on either mode starts a fresh board
